Welcome!
I am now an assistant professor in School of Mathematics and Statistics, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China. I defended my PhD in 2020 at Peking University under Dayue Chen(陈大岳). After that, I worked as a Post-Doc at Inria-Lille with Marielle Simon and Clément Erignoux from 2020 to 2022.
My research interests are interacting particle systems and hydrodynamic limits.
